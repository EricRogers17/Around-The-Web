Welcome to Around The Web

Around The Web is a simple board game that allows you to advance based on the number you roll.

Rules: Advance by rolling a six sided die. When you land on certain spaces you must answer a simple riddle. Get it wrong, and you must go back to start. Land on a # symbol, move forward an additional two spaces. Land on a @ symbol, move back three spaces. If you land on a green space, the internet troll will eat you and you lose a turn. First person to the end wins!
